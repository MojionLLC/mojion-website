FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a custom AI voice agent?

Most engagements ship in 2 to 4 weeks. Our standard phases are Discovery (about 2 days), Design and Prototype (about 3 days), Build and Integration (about 1 week), and Launch and Monitoring (a 2-week supervised live period).

Do I own the AI voice agent you build?

Yes. We design every engagement so the client owns the code, prompts, workflows, and integrations. No vendor lock-in, no surprise licensing. You can choose monthly hosting and monitoring or a full buyout with optional maintenance.

What does a custom AI receptionist cost?

Every engagement is priced per-client because outcomes and goals vary widely — call volume, integrations, compliance requirements, and the level of human-in-the-loop oversight all shape the scope. We don't publish price tiers because the right system for a small home services team isn't the right system for a multi-location equipment dealer. After a free Discovery call, we send a fixed-scope quote tied to the outcomes you actually want.

How is a custom MoJion build different from Synthflow, Smith.ai, or Frontdesk?

Three differences. First, you own the system — we don't rent you a seat on our platform. Second, our architecture restricts the AI to language and classification while deterministic workflows enforce business outcomes, so failures are isolated and auditable. Third, accountability is built in: E&O insurance, structured logging, and human-in-the-loop escalation are first-class behaviors, not afterthoughts.

Is your AI HIPAA-compliant?

Our build patterns are HIPAA-ready: BAA-eligible vendor selection, PHI minimization, audit logging, and access controls. Final compliance depends on your specific data flows, which we map during Discovery.

What happens when the AI can't handle a call?

Escalation is designed in from day one. The agent transfers to a human, triggers a Slack alert with the full call context and recording, or schedules a callback — depending on the use case. The AI never silently fails.

Which CRMs and tools do you integrate with?

Anything with an API. HubSpot is our most common CRM integration. We also routinely connect to Google Sheets, Slack, Twilio, Stripe, calendar systems, and custom databases through n8n.
